Harvverse: The Agricultural DeFi Network State 🌿🌐
Harvverse is a pioneering "phygital" platform that connects physical farming with the digital economy. We leverage blockchain, IoT, and the metaverse to empower producers and provide investors with traceable, high-impact opportunities in sustainable agriculture.

🏗️ Technical Architecture: Phygital Ecosystem
Our infrastructure is organized into four core modules represented in this repository:

Blockchain Core (blockchain-core): Built on Base (Ethereum L2), managing the logic for Dynamic NFTs that represent physical or virtual farm parcels.

Data Engine (data-engine): Processes real-time telemetry from Siucom IoT sensors, including weather conditions, soil moisture, and crop yields.

Impact Registry (impact-registry): Algorithms for Carbon Credit certification and the CIC (Connect, Innovate, Grow) protocol for female farmer empowerment.

Metaverse Assets (metaverse-assets): Interactive configurations for Saja Labs, our physical and virtual spaces for agricultural R&D and immersive education.

🌍 Social & Environmental Impact
Traceability: NFTs ensure total transparency in the origin and quality of agricultural products.

Climate Action: Targeting a 25% reduction in carbon footprints through sustainable practices and verifiable carbon sequestration.

Gender Equity: Aiming for 35% female participation by 2025, providing specialized training in blockchain and agricultural data analysis.

📍 Traction & Roadmap
Validation: Award-winning platform, including the Golden Fintech DeFi 2024 at Fintech Américas.

Scaling: Over 100 farms onboarded in 2024, with a goal of reaching 10,000 connected farms worldwide by 2030.

Global Footprint: Expanding to climate clusters in Brazil, Colombia, India, and Ethiopia.
