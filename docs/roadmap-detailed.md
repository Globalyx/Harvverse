🚀 Harvverse Strategic Roadmap: From MVP to Global Scale
Harvverse follows a high-efficiency Scale Up Methodology, prioritizing CAPEX efficiency and technical agility. Our roadmap is divided into four strategic phases:

Phase 1: Strategy, Preparation & Onboarding (15% Allocation)

Status: COMPLETED.


Hardening: Refined Whitepaper, Pitch Deck, and technical documentation focusing on Play-to-Earn and Gold Standard NFTs.


MVP Express: Playable build, cross-platform NFT minting, and automated bounty-feedback systems.


Governance: Preliminary DAO (Minimum Viable DAO) and basic compliance framework established.

Phase 2: Community Building & Diversity (25% Allocation)

Status: IN PROGRESS.


Engagement: Multichannel management (Discord, Telegram, X) and recruitment of "Legendary Testers" and inclusive DAO ambassadors.


Dynamic Quests: Implementation of "Learn-to-Earn" missions, badges, and NFT incentives based on social participation.

Phase 3: Go-To-Market & Viralization (35% Allocation)

Status: PENDING FUNDING.


DeFi Activation: Liquidity mining, custom pools, and competitive farming events.


Launch Events: Gamified crowdfunding, P2E milestones, and integration with VISA/fiat-cashback systems.

Phase 4: Sustainability & DAO Evolution (25% Allocation)

Status: FUTURE VISION.


Full Automation: On-chain reward streams for bug bounties and learning milestones.


Reporting: Transparent dashboards for metrics (liquidity, community, diversity) and quarterly reporting to the DAO
